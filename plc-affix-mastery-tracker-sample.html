Teacher's Intervention Guide
Targeted Affix Instruction for Reading Comprehension
CCSS: 3.RC.5, 3.RF.4, & 3.W.3
Implementation Guidelines
Intervention Rigor Progression
Lessons 1–5: High scaffolding (tracing, cut/paste)
Lessons 6–10: Moderate scaffolding (sentence starters)
Lessons 11–14: Minimal scaffolding (open-ended prompts)
Implementation Steps
1. Diagnostic Tool: Identify which affixes students struggle with during reading
2. Targeted Instruction: Group students by mastery level (Below, Approaching, At, Above)
3. Progress Monitoring: Reassess after teaching specific affix groups
Lesson Structure
Each lesson follows a consistent structure:
1. Affix Warm-Up - Review target affixes, meanings, and example words
2. Main Idea Exploration - Identify main idea and key details in passage
3. Scaffolded Writing Activity - Apply target affixes in writing
4. Progress Monitoring - Track mastery of affixes and comprehension skills
